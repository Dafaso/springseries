Ride Details

Week 1.

Palisades Park Ride Details

Distance: approximately 26 miles.

Start time and location will be sent by email to all registered riders the week of the ride.

After a brief introduction we will split into loose groups to ride up the West Side Bike Path and over the George Washington Bridge. You may travel between groups as you evaluate different riding styles and speeds. From there, we will have a quick stop at Strictly Bicycles in Englewood NJ before continuing on 9W to Alpine and the entrance to the Palisades Park. We will have a rest stop at the 'Cop Shop' (Palisades Police Station) in the park before returning home. The return will split - For those that are 'hill adverse', we will return on 9W. Or for those that are ready for more of a challenge we will return through Palisades Park - a beautiful but hilly ride! This is a 2 - 3 hour ride.

Week 2.

Classic Nyack Ride Details

Distance: approximately 45 miles.

Start time and location will be sent by email to all registered riders the week of the ride.

After a brief introduction we will split into groups - and ride up the West Side Bike Path and over the George Washington Bridge. Once in NJ we will have a quick stop at Strictly Bicycles before continuing on to the entrance to the Palisades Park for a quick rest stop if needed. From there Steady Eddies will travel to Piermont while the Speed Freaks will hit the town of Nyack before turning around and meeting up with the Eddies for a lunch stop in the town of Piermont.
